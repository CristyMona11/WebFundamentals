My Favorite Place in the World, Costa Rica

Costa Rica is my favorite place in the world for multiple reasons. I have been lucky enough to travel to over fifteen countries, while working on some coding at the same time. Tamarindo, Costa Rica is by far my favorite city in the world thus far. It provides the best environment to code due to its:
  • Phenomenal Weather
  • Friendly, Awesome Locals
  • Great Surfing
  • Amazing Food, Drinks
  • Always Get A Great Tan
  • The Most Positive Vibes with Great Energy
